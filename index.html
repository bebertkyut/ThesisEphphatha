Ephphatha:



A Voice to American Sign Language Translator, aiding the Church of God Dasmariñas Deaf Ministry Fellowship

About me

Ephphatha is a revolutionary system that translates spoken language into American Sign Language (ASL). Designed to aid the Church of God Dasmariñas Deaf Ministry Fellowship, Ephphatha empowers Deaf individuals by providing real-time ASL gestures for spoken words.

With a user-friendly interface, users can customize signing speed and style, ensuring a seamless integration into the church environment. Ephphatha is the result of collaboration between software engineers, designers, and ASL experts, dedicated to making communication inclusive and accessible.
